Quick Tips
Adding videos: Go to any section item, click Videos, and paste a YouTube or Vimeo link. The site embeds it automatically.
Images: Upload JPG or PNG. Portraits should be square or vertical. Section images should be landscape (16:9).
News articles: Set the Published toggle to ON when ready. Drafts are saved but hidden from the public site.
Campaigns: Mark each campaign as Current or Historical — the site groups them accordingly.
